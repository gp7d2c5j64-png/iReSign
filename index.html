Acro mode — no self-leveling. The quad holds whatever angle you set.

ARM first (throttle must be at zero — safety interlock). Tap ARM (or press Space) anytime to disarm and cut motors instantly.

Fly through the gold gates down the street. Land gently (under ~4.5 m/s) to touch down safely — hit harder and you'll crash and need to reset.

Keyboard: throttle W/S, yaw A/D, pitch ↑/↓, roll ←/→, arm Space, reset R.
