TinyWhoop Lap RF

Point your phone or webcam at a gate. Each time your whoop flies through the box on screen, it counts one lap — and a voice calls out your time.

This app needs your camera to see the gate. Nothing is recorded or uploaded — all detection runs on your device.

Install to your Home Screen: tap the Share icon, then Add to Home Screen. It then opens full-screen like a native app.
